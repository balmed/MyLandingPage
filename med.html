e_Learning platforms

Existing eLearning platforms often lack comprehensive content and accessibility. This project seeks to address these gaps by providing a structured and accessible eLearning platform. While advanced topics may not be covered extensively, the platform aims to cater to learners of all levels, offering a diverse range of courses. Access to a smartphone and PC or internet connection is essential for users.
