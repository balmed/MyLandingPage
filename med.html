Key Features

Application :

This part comes to demonstrate some interface and functionality of our application, especially those that appear very important. When the visitor arrives on our application, he will be on the home page.

Student :

Once the student is accepted by the system [registration form], as already explained, he must authenticate [authentication form] in the same home page with his CIN and password Afterwards he goes to his personal space which offers him the possibility to take advantage of and consult the online courses as well as he can contact other users by sending them messages.take coursese...

Teacher :

After authentication, the application directs the connected teacher to his personal space, which offers him the possibility of adding and sending the desired course and putting it online, as he can from his space to connect others users..
